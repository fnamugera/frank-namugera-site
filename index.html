About

Frank Namugera is a Lecturer at the School of Statistics and Planning, Makerere University, where he is affiliated with the Statistical Computing Group.

He holds a PhD from Makerere University.

"Questions that begin in statistical mechanics or biology, worked out rigorously — and usually on a graph."

Research Focus

Stochastic processes on networks

His work focuses on the rigorous mathematical theory of stochastic processes, often motivated by questions arising from statistical mechanics or biology, and frequently set on an underlying network structure.
